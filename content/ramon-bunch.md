Ramon Bunch joined the board of Open City Advocates in 2023. Ramon was the first client ever served by our Co-Founders when he joined their student-led organization in 2003. He has consistently served as a trusted advisor for over two decades and has extensive lived experience in DC's juvenile and child welfare systems, the DC Jail, and the Federal Bureau of Prisons.
