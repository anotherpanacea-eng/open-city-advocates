Kristen Powell is a staff attorney at Open City Advocates. Kristen is passionate about advocating for marginalized communities and committed to providing holistic advocacy. Before joining Open City Advocates, Kristen was a staff attorney at Maryland Legal Aid litigating on behalf of clients experiencing poverty. Kristen practiced in a variety of areas, including divorce and custody, civil protection orders, debt collection, employment, housing, and criminal record expungement. Before Maryland Legal Aid, Kristen was a law clerk on the Appellate Court of Maryland.

Kristen received her JD from American University Washington College of Law, where she was a criminal law research assistant. Kristen received her BA from Michigan State University, where she studied Political Science with a concentration in pre-law.

Kristen is licensed to practice in the District of Columbia and Maryland.
