*"Every child deserves to be seen, heard, and valued — not locked away and forgotten."* <small class="pending">⟦Whitney Louchheim, via OCA's 2024 appeal letter — pending OCA sign-off⟧</small>

For twenty years, a small team has delivered outsized impact: holistic legal representation and long-term mentoring for 60–70 young people a year, and reforms, like the right to counsel throughout commitment, that reach every committed youth in the District.

## Where your support goes

Your gift funds representation at placement decisions and review hearings, six-year mentoring relationships, reentry support at release, and the policy work in our [advocacy archive](/policy-advocacy/).

- **[Donate](https://opencityadvocates.app.neoncrm.com/forms/donation-form)**: directly funds client representation and reentry support
- **[Subscribe](https://opencityadvocates.app.neoncrm.com/subscribe.jsp)**: follow the work
- **Partner with us**: [info@opencityadvocates.org](mailto:info@opencityadvocates.org)
