## Know your rights: you have the right to a lawyer

If you are committed to DYRS, **you have the right to a lawyer** — even during your commitment. This lawyer is called a post-commitment lawyer. They are *your* lawyer; they do not work for the court or DYRS.

Are you worried about your next placement? Do you need help talking with DYRS staff? Are you having trouble with school? Medication? Safety? **A lawyer can help.**

**Not sure if you're committed?** Ask your trial attorney. If your trial is finished and you are still at YSC or New Beginnings, ask a care coordinator or case worker.

### To sign up for a post-commitment lawyer — or if you have questions

- Contact Open City Advocates: [info@opencityadvocates.org](mailto:info@opencityadvocates.org) or **(202) 678-9001**
- Or the Public Defender Service: **(202) 576-8368**
